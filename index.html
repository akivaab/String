Letter tiles will fall from the top of the screen.

Connect the letters to form words and delete the tiles. They may be connected horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Words must be at least 3 letters long - the longer the word, the more points you earn!

Once the entire screen is filled with tiles, the game ends.
